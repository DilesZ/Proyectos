# Execution Plan: Agencia de Influencers de IA

## 1. Key Milestones
- **Phase 1: Identity Genesis (Week 1)**
    - Creation of the "Master Image" (Face Reference).
    - Setup of social media profiles (Instagram, TikTok).
    - Landing page/Linktree setup for conversion.
- **Phase 2: Content Factory Setup (Week 2)**
    - Generation of first 30 days of image content (selfies, lifestyle, professional).
    - Creation of first 5 "Viral Reels" using video animation tools.
    - Setup of Fanvue profile and initial exclusive content vault.
- **Phase 3: Launch & Funnel (Week 3)**
    - Opening of the subscription model.
    - Start of automated traffic strategy (following/interaction scripts).
    - Implementation of automated AI chat responses on Fanvue.
- **Phase 4: Optimization & Scaling (Month 1+)**
    - Analysis of conversion rates.
    - Introduction of PPV (Pay-Per-View) campaigns.
    - Creation of a second influencer to diversify the portfolio.

## 2. Necessary Resources
- **Visual Tools**: Nano Banana Pro (Images), Grock/Meta AI (Video), One 2.2 Anime (Motion).
- **Audio Tools**: ElevenLabs or Fish Audio for voice consistency.
- **Platforms**: Fanvue (Monetization), Instagram/TikTok (Traffic).
- **Automation**: AI Chat integration for Fanvue messaging.
- **Human/Dev Time**: 10-15 hours/week for strategy and prompt engineering.

## 3. Comprehensive Timeline
- **Days 1-3**: Persona design and voice cloning.
- **Days 4-7**: Social media infrastructure and content warm-up.
- **Days 8-14**: Mass content production (Images + Reels).
- **Day 15**: Official launch of the Fanvue subscription.
- **Days 16-30**: Aggressive traffic generation and first payout.

## 4. Risk Mitigation
- **Platform Bans**: Maintain backup profiles and diversify traffic sources.
- **Quality Decay**: Regular fine-tuning of prompts to ensure visual consistency as AI models update.
